· Wristlight ·

Background + Overview

Wristlight is a LED ﬂash light that is designed for kids around the age of 6 and goes home alone when the sun is down. Kids are most of the time crazy when playing, a wearable ﬂashlight can prevent them from losing it, however imagine the running, falling, rolling during kids’ playtime, it’s hard to keep an item as exquisite as a ﬂashlight in good condition.

Therefore, a rugged and durable ﬂashlight is needed to stand-up to the “harsh” conditions it goes through. But it’s important to keep in mind that Wristlight is used as a wristband, so the surface is coated to prevent harm caused by rugged material to kids, and provide waterproof ability for cleaning.

Persona + process

Our persona Mike is a 6-year-old boy who is very outgoing and loves role-play. He often acts superheroes and villains with his friends, fighting with each other in playground until the sun goes down. His parents worry that he might get hurt due to the rugged road on the way home, so they brought Mike a flash light to help him see the road even when it’s dark outside. Mike can wear the flashlight like a wristband, so he can play freely without worrying about losing the flashlight.

We wanted to make a flashlight that does not get lost easily, therefore we came up with the idea of a flashlight in the form of a wristband. At first, the flashlight is in a round shape, then we decide to open it so the light can be more directed and we adjusted the angle at the joint so the light shines forward.

Key Features

Wristlight’s LED lights are located at the joint surrounding a piece of small magnet, it turns on when the joint breaks by the twisting action causing the two magnets on both side of the joint to disconnect.

The two magnets attached to each other will signal the lights to turn off, and breaking the loop will turn the lights on.

User put Wristlight on by twisting the flashlight around the axis, that opens the flashlight so user can put his wrist in the concave then twist the flashlight back to its wristband form.

When Wristligt is taken off from wrist, user can hold it in the middle, there are slots for better grip and thumb rest. The LED lights shines at an angle due to the angled cut at the joint, therefore when holding the flashlight upright, the light shines forward.
